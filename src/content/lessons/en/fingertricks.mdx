Speed comes from fingertips, not wrists. The two highest-impact fingertricks: index-finger U and U' (snap with the side of your finger), and middle-finger M slices.

### Mechanics

- `U`: flick with your left index finger from below the top layer.
- `U'`: flick with your right index finger.
- `R` / `R'`: push with right index/middle finger — never wrist-rotate.
- `F`: pinch and roll, don't rotate the whole hand.

<div class="tip">
  Drill: alternate `R U R' U'` at increasing speed with a metronome. Eventually you'll hit 8+ TPS.
</div>
