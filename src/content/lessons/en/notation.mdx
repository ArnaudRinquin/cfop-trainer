Every algorithm you'll learn is just letters: `R U R' U'`. Master the alphabet and the whole CFOP method opens up.

### Six faces, six letters

Hold the cube with one color on top. The six faces are: _R_ight, _L_eft, _U_p, _D_own, _F_ront, _B_ack. A bare letter means a clockwise quarter-turn of that face — clockwise as if you were staring straight at it.

### Modifiers

- `R` = right face clockwise (90°)
- `R'` ("R prime") = right face counter-clockwise
- `R2` = right face half-turn (180°). Direction doesn't matter.

### Rotations and slices

- `x y z` rotate the whole cube around the R, U, F axes
- `r l u d f b` (lowercase) are _wide_ turns — two layers at once
- `M` is the middle slice between R and L (turns with L direction)

<div class="tip">
  Drill: pick up your cube and perform `R U R' U'` six times. You should land back on the start position. This sequence is called the _sexy move_ and you'll do it more than anything else.
</div>
